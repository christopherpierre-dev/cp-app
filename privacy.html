Privacy Policy

Communication Properly (CP)
Effective date: July 13, 2026
Developed by CP Labs Inc. — Christopher Pierre

1. Information We Collect

Communication Properly processes voice audio in real time to provide translation services. The app may collect:

  • Voice audio input during active translation sessions
  • Selected language preferences stored locally on your device
  • Azure API credentials you choose to store locally on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

Voice audio is sent to Microsoft Azure Cognitive Services solely to perform real-time speech translation. We do not store, sell, or share your audio recordings. Language preferences and API credentials are stored only on your device using browser localStorage and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

CP uses Microsoft Azure Cognitive Services (Speech) to perform translations. Your voice data is processed according to Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain any voice recordings or personal data on our servers. All locally stored preferences can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app data.

5. Children's Privacy

Communication Properly is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
