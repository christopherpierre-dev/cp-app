Privacy Policy

Equivox (formerly Communication Properly)
Effective date: July 15, 2026
Published by CREDIA INC.

1. Information We Process

Equivox processes voice audio in real time to provide translation services. The app processes:

  • Voice audio input from your microphone, only during active translation sessions you start
  • Text transcripts and translations of what is said during a session
  • Selected language preferences, stored locally on your device

Microphone access is only active while you hold or toggle the microphone button, and you can revoke it at any time in your browser or device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

Voice audio is sent to Microsoft Azure AI Speech services solely to perform real-time speech recognition and translation. We do not store, sell, or share your audio recordings. Language preferences are stored only on your device and are never used for advertising.

3. Remote Call Mode

When you use Remote Call, the text of what you say (original and translation) is relayed between participants through a CP relay server. This relay is transient: messages pass through the server to the other participants in your room and are not stored, logged, or retained. Rooms are identified by short random codes and are automatically deleted after a period of inactivity. All connections use encrypted transport (HTTPS/WSS). The Equivox server also issues short-lived access tokens so that the app can reach Azure AI Speech securely — no Azure credentials are ever present on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Equivox uses Microsoft Azure AI Speech to perform speech recognition, translation, and voice synthesis. Your voice data is processed according to Microsoft's Privacy Statement. The CP relay server is hosted on Render.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain voice recordings, transcripts, or personal data on our servers. All locally stored preferences can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app data.

6. Children's Privacy

Equivox is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time — for example, when new features such as optional voice preservation are introduced (any such feature will require your explicit consent before any voice profile is created). Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
